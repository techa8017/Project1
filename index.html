Electrical Works in Buildings

Explore the essential electrical systems that power our modern architecture — from wiring and lighting to smart technologies.

Introduction

Electrical works in buildings ensure the safe and efficient distribution of power. They include wiring, lighting, safety systems, and smart automation that improve comfort and functionality.

Wiring & Installation

Wiring connects the entire electrical system — from the main panel to outlets, lighting, and smart devices. Proper planning ensures safety, energy efficiency, and easy maintenance.

Safety & Protection

Safety systems like circuit breakers, grounding, and insulation prevent electrical hazards and ensure a reliable power supply throughout the building.
