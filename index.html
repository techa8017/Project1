Electrical Works in Buildings

Intro Lighting Wiring Safety Systems

Introduction

Electrical works are one of the most essential stages in building construction. They include the design and installation of lighting systems, power distribution, and safety systems such as fire alarms and security networks.

Lighting Systems

Lighting systems are crucial for both aesthetics and functionality in architecture. A good lighting design ensures comfort, efficiency, and energy savings for building interiors and exteriors.

Electrical Wiring

Electrical wiring involves all cables, conduits, switches, and sockets that distribute electricity safely through the building. Proper design and installation reduce hazards and ensure functionality.

Safety and Protection Systems

Safety systems include fire alarms, CCTV surveillance, and access control systems. They are vital to protect both occupants and assets in modern architectural designs.
